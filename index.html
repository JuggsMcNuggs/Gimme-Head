Rotate Your Phone

This game is played in landscape. Turn your phone sideways, then tap the button below.

How To Play

1. Hold your phone to your forehead with the screen facing out so everyone else can see the word.

2. Ask the room yes or no questions to figure out what's on your head.

3. "Am I a person?" "Can you eat me?" "Am I something dirty?" — anything goes.

4. When you guess it right, someone taps got it for you. If you're stuck, tap skip.

5. Pass to the next player. No score, no timer, just vibes.
